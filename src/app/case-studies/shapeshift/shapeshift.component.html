The Project

Shapeshift is a concept for a gender neutral makeup subscription service with the mission of disrupting the accepted ideas about makeup and make it accessible for all. It aims to be subversive yet sleek, and provides a transformative experience for people who are seeking to break with stereotypes.

Icon & Logo
